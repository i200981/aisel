About AISEL

AiSEL is a Collaborative research endeavour by Dr. Khubaib Amjad Alam and Dr. Haroon Mahmood from Al Ain University (UAE) and Dr. Nadeem Abbas from Linnaeus University, Sweden. We are closely working as a research group (AISEL) that strives to achieve research excellence in the field of software engineering. My research group works in two larger dimensions:

  • Software Engineering
  • Operations research & AI

Most of the research activity seeks convergence of these two dimensions. More specifically, we seek applications of AI/ML, granular computing, soft computing, optimization algorithms, and DSS to solve complex software engineering problems.

Currently, AISEL division members are working on Software engineering-centric mining, knowledge extraction, and classification problems on diverse platforms and repositories. AISEL members are harnessing the unmatched power of deep learning models, transformer-based approaches, advanced NLP techniques, and language models (LLMs) to solve complex and non-trivial problems in software engineering. AISEL members are also working on developing innovative evolutionary computation algorithms, Approximate reasoning & Fuzzy Decision support systems for interesting selection, ranking, and scheduling problems in the field of software engineering. In 2024, we have got acceptance from Top Tier software Engineering venues including, ICSE, ECSA, ASE, QRS to name a few. Several contributions are under review in reputed venues.
