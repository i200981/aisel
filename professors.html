Dr. Khubaib Amjad Alam

Dr. Khubaib Amjad Alam is an Associate Professor and Head of Software Engineering and AI-driven Software Engineering Lab (AISEL). He holds a Ph.D. in Software Engineering from the University of Malaya and has published extensively in top-tier journals and conferences. His research focuses on Automated Software Engineering, Evidence-based Software Engineering, Software Testing, and Mathematical Optimization. Dr. Khubaib has supervised numerous Masters Theses and Capstone Projects. His teaching philosophy combines inclusive learning, active engagement, and project-based assessments.

Dr. Nadeem Abbas

Dr. Nadeem Abbas is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Media Technology at Linnaeus University, Sweden. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer and Information Science and specializes in Self-Adaptive Software Systems, Software Product Lines, Software Reuse, and Software Architecture. His research focuses on improving software system flexibility, reusability, and efficiency. Dr. Abbas has published extensively in top-tier journals and conferences, contributing valuable insights into the evolution and design of complex software systems. He is dedicated to advancing the field of software engineering through both research and teaching.

Dr. Haroon Mahmood

With numerous publications in high-impact journals and conferences, Dr. Haroon is dedicated to advancing knowledge in software engineering practices and research.
